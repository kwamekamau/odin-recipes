4-Ingredient Vegan Coconut Bacon

Description

If plant-based eating has you nostalgic for bacon, you're in luck. This recipe transforms coconut flakes into salty. smoky, bacon-like bits that are delicious sprinkled over a salad, tucked into a baked potato, or—why not?—on a maple-flavored donut. Make sure to use unsweetened coconut.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Thrive Market Organic Coconut Chips
  • 4 tablespoons Thrive Market Organic Coconut Aminos
  • 2 tablespoons liquid smoke
  • Drizzle of Thrive Market Organic Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, combine all ingredients until coconut chips are completely coated. Transfer the coconut chips to the baking sheet in an even layer.
  3. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes, tossing occasionally. Let cool, then store in the fridge for up to 7 days or in the freezer indefinitely.
